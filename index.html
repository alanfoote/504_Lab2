Books and White Halls: Libaries and Hospitals


This interactive map shows the locations of public libaries in Tacoma and informs the user of the closet hospital in proximity to the library you might be at. Click on any of the book icons to learn what library exists there and what hospital is closest to it. Click on the medical crosses to learn more about the hospitals of Tacoma.




Data source: Emma Slager, School of Urban Studies, University of Washington
